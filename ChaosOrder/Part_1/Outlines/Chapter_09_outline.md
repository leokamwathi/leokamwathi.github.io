## Essence
June finds an anonymous, artful poem in her locker. The Trio treats it as a puzzle, building investigative intimacy while June wrestles with being seen and keeping consent at the center.

## Main Story

### Essence
A mystery note turns rooftop bylaws into practice—privacy, consent, and quiet collaboration—deepening the trio’s trust and giving June a safe way to be a subject, not just an observer.
