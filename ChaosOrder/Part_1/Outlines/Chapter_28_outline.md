## Essence
The band finally has a breakthrough, blending styles into a unique song. Emotional protocols and form (haiku cadence → lyric) convert conflict into composition. In parallel, Alex struggles to finish his secret song about Jayden; and Dexter’s well‑meant gadget gift to Tracy misfires, escalating intimacy risk.
