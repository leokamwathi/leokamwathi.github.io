## Arc Summary

- The story moves from first-month social formation into a sharper pressure pattern built around systems, prestige, labor, and emotional exposure.
- W.E.I.R.D turns future-fit, observation, and institutional validation into a live force that affects Dex, June, and Kate differently.
- Academic Pal's becomes operational and starts reshaping who has to spend time with whom, which slowly pulls Prita into the ensemble as more than a system-node.
- The Great Waterfall Bake-Off grounds the story back in town life and reveals Tracy's craft, labor, and authorship more clearly than earlier weeks did.
- Midterm week strips away performance bandwidth and reveals coping styles under reduced energy rather than high-event drama.
- June's family pressure becomes openly legible during the Lyu dinner, and the admirer thread escalates from one poem to two while still refusing easy interpretation.
- The Halloween party acts as a truth amplifier rather than a resolution point: masks, image, safety, jealousy, and performance all become harder to keep separate.
- By the end of Week 8, the ensemble is emotionally messier but also more native to itself. Several people are more visible, but almost nobody is more settled.

- Nikole: continues shaping June's emotional field through visibility and observational presence, even when not fully centered.
