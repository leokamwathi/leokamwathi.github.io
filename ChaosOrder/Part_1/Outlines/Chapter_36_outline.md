## Essence
One night, the long‑dormant lighthouse cuts through the dark. The town gasps. June calls it a miracle; Dexter keeps his secret project quiet; the group lets awe be shared without spectacle. Elsewhere, Martin’s deception about his ankle underscores the cost of choosing a different path.
