## Essence
A scare with Joseph intensifies Luciano’s push toward soccer. Alone later, Martin discovers solace in his mother’s tennis photos and quietly claims a thread of her legacy—seed of divergence kept private.

## Main Story

### Essence
Martin is split between obligation and affinity. Private ritual (ball against wall, photo in wallet) begins converting pressure into chosen identity.
