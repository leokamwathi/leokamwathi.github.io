# Chapter 8: The Wager

- Calendar Period: Saturday, September 6 (Afternoon–Evening)
- Special Events: Skatepark wager; Blind dates terms set
- Locations: Waterfall Skatepark; Bleachers; Arcade alley (post‑match); Back gate (flashback)
- POV Character: Alex
- Characters (Main Story): Alex; Dexter; Finn; Gabriel; Jayden (periphery)
- Items/Motifs in Play: Stakes‑as‑banter; “No posts” bylaw tension; Run rules; Stopwatch/phone timer; Heelflip bail; Boardslide clean execution

## Essence
Cornered by a Jayden sighting, Alex deflects into bravado—accepting a skate wager with Finn he can’t win. The loss binds him (and an unwilling Dexter) to blind dates, turning performance into consequences.

## Main Story

### Essence
Mask writes checks: Alex’s spectacle converts panic into binding stakes, collateralizing friendship and tightening future social exposure.

### Beats (time‑bucketed)
- (Afternoon — Sat, Sep 6, Skatepark gate): Jayden appears at the entrance; Alex’s posture stiffens. He jokes fear away at Finn’s rail, baiting a challenge to redirect attention. Gabriel agrees to judge. [Why: deflect trigger → control scene]
- (Afternoon — Sat, Sep 6, Bleachers): Terms set: two 45‑second lines, best run wins; loser accepts two blind dates chosen by winners. Alex taps Dexter as his “coach” to save face; Dexter blanches but stays. [Continuity → run rules] [Setup → Ch11 Arrangement]
- (Afternoon — Sat, Sep 6, Park run 1): Alex goes big early; lands a messy kickflip off the funbox, bails on the manual out. Crowd oohs. Gabriel’s count is even. [Motif → Performance impulse]
- (Afternoon — Sat, Sep 6, Park run 2): Finn stays clean and technical, sticks a boardslide to 180 out; Alex overreaches on a heelflip off the ledge and eats it. Silence, then cheers. [Loss]
- (Afternoon — Sat, Sep 6, Park center): Gabriel calls it: Finn wins. Finn and Gabriel lock the wager; Dexter’s name is written into the terms. Alex laughs too loudly, pretending it was nothing; fear flickers under the bit. [Consequence locked]
- (Evening — Sat, Sep 6, Arcade alley): Alex and Dexter step aside; texts fly to “set it up next weekend.” Alex reasserts: “No posts.” Dexter nods, still stunned; they agree to hold the rooftop rule even off‑roof. [Bylaw echo]

### Key Character Moments
- Alex: Converts panic into spectacle; accepts public loss; commits to consequences that will complicate private truth. [Motif → Stakes‑as‑banter]
- Dexter: Dragged into exposure by proximity to Alex; learns the cost of another’s mask. [Setup → Ch11]
- Finn: Prefers clean execution over flash; enjoys leverage without cruelty. [Rival silhouette]
- Gabriel: Fair arbiter; his word makes the bet real. [Authority of peers]
- Jayden: Wordless catalyst; presence alone spikes Alex’s avoidance. [Thread → unresolved charge]
