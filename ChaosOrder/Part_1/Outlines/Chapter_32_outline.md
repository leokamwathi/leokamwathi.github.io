## Main Story

### Essence

- (Evening — 7:34 PM, Stairwell → Rooftop R1): June and Nikole slip out. Phones face‑down; “Kept?” “Kept.” Brief rooftop slow dance. Stealth window; keeper extension logged to Continuity Ledger (per Ch22). No photos. [Private claim]

- (Evening — 7:46 PM, Dance floor): Kate and Martin catch each other’s eyes over partners; the strain in their current pairings is palpable. Mackenzie doubles down on smile; Kate’s smile thins, then steadies. [Pressure]
- (Evening — 7:52 PM, Photo booth corner): Dexter and Tracy debrief with a glance—no label, but the shared beat remains. Tracy tucks a curl; Dexter looks at her hands, not her face, to calm. [Afterglow]
- (Evening — 8:03 PM, Gym exit): Group regathers; chaperone thanks. Rooftop off‑limits tonight except the logged brief. Diner plan forms (→ Ch33). [Close]
