## Essence
During a heated rehearsal, Kate says, “Just be useful for once,” inadvertently echoing abusive language from Alex’s past. Alex’s visceral reaction cracks group cohesion, revealing the need for emotional protocols.

## Main Story

### Essence
Careless language can wound; bands need rules for feelings as much as for sound.
