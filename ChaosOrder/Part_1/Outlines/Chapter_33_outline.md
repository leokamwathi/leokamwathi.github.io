## Essence
Buoyed by the dance, June shares her relationship with Nikole at the diner. The friends accept them plainly, turning secrecy into community. Small gestures—not spectacle—cement the new bond.

## Main Story

### Essence
Acceptance is quiet joy practiced together.

### Beats (time‑bucketed)

- (Evening — 9:19 PM, Diner booth): Nikole shares the patch story in two sentences; June places the patch on the table; everyone touches it once, then it goes back on June’s jacket. No photos. [Ritual]
- (Evening — 9:27 PM, Counter): Mr. Thompson pays for a plate of fries and sends it to their table with a nod—community blessing without intrusion. [Community]
- (Evening — 9:34 PM, Booth): Mackenzie passes by, offers a neutral “Congrats on the show.” Kate’s jaw tightens, then she lets it go. June scribbles a compass‑star on a napkin and tapes it near the edge. [Tension managed]

- (Evening — 9:52 PM, Parking lot): Group hug is skipped by choice; instead, pairs tap shoulders. June and Nikole breathe once together; Alex smiles at the sky. [Closure]
